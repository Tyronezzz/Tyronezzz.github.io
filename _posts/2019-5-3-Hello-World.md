### Motivation

Sometimes it can be hard to find someone having same interests with you. When you want to try a new Itlian restaurant and your friends do not like Itlian food, you can create a post in this application, then other people who also want to try this restaurant will join you. Whatever you want to do, you can post on JOIN ME app to find a friend to join you.
