### Motivation

Sometimes it can be hard to find someone who has the same interest as you. For example, if you want to try a new Itlian restaurant while your friends do not like Itlian food, you can create a post in this application, then other people who also want to try this restaurant will join you. Whatever you want to do, you can post on JoinMe app to find a friend to join you.
